How to Preview Your LinkedIn Post

From blank page to pixel-perfect preview in four simple steps — then screenshot and share with your team or clients.

01

Choose Post Type

Select from five post types: text, image, document carousel, link preview, or poll. Each renders with accurate LinkedIn UI components.

02

Write Your Content

Type your post text with hashtags, mentions, and line breaks. The preview updates live as you write — no refresh needed.

03

Customize Details

Set your name, title, reactions, comment counts, connection degree, and toggle features like promoted label and "see more" truncation.

04

Screenshot & Share

Click the Screenshot button to instantly download a PNG. Share with your team for approvals, or use in presentations and reports.

What Is This?

The Most Accurate LinkedIn Post Preview Available

Your LinkedIn post is more than words — it's layout, first impressions, hashtag visibility, image framing, and how your name and title appear next to your content. All of that shapes engagement before a single person reads your copy.

TweetPreview's LinkedIn Post Preview renders your post in pixel-accurate LinkedIn UI for both dark and light modes — so what you build in the composer is exactly what your connections see in their feed.

The screenshot button converts the live preview directly into a downloadable PNG. Share it with your team for approval, add it to a pitch deck, or keep it as a record of your campaign content — all without logging into LinkedIn.

  • Real-time rendering — no submit button, no reload
  • 5 post types: text, image, document, link preview, poll
  • Dark mode and light mode — pixel-accurate LinkedIn styling
  • One-click PNG screenshot download via html2canvas
  • All 6 LinkedIn reactions with custom counts
  • Connection degree badge (1st, 2nd, 3rd)
  • Custom comments with name, title & avatar
  • Promoted / Sponsored label toggle
  • See more text truncation toggle
  • 100% private — zero data stored, zero sent to servers
