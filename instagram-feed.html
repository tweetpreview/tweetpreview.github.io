How to Preview Your Instagram Post

Four steps from idea to pixel-perfect Instagram preview — screenshot for approvals or content planning.

01

Write Your Caption

Type your caption with hashtags, mentions, and emojis. Toggle between full caption or truncated "…more" — just like the real Instagram app shows it.

02

Upload Your Photo

Drag & drop or click to upload any image. Choose from square (1:1), portrait (4:5), or landscape (1.91:1) to see exactly how it crops on your feed.

03

Customize Every Detail

Set likes, location tag, story ring, verified badge, carousel indicators, comments, liked/saved states — then toggle dark or light mode instantly.

04

Screenshot & Share

Click Screenshot to instantly download a high-res 2× PNG. Share with clients or your team for approvals — no Instagram login ever needed.

What Is This?

The Most Accurate Instagram Post Preview Available

Before you hit publish, you need to know: Does the caption get cut off at "…more"? Do hashtags look cluttered? Is the 4:5 crop right? How does your palette look in dark mode vs light?

TweetPreview's Instagram Feed Post Preview renders your post using pixel-accurate Instagram UI — the exact fonts, spacing, action icons, story rings, and caption layout your followers see — in both dark and light mode, on every aspect ratio.

The one-click Screenshot button (powered by html2canvas) renders a downloadable PNG at 2× retina resolution. Share with your team, drop into a content calendar, or present to a client — all without logging into Instagram.

  • Real-time caption rendering with hashtag & mention highlighting
  • Image upload with 3 aspect ratios: 1:1, 4:5, and 1.91:1
  • Pixel-accurate dark mode and light mode Instagram UI
  • One-click PNG screenshot download at 2× retina quality
  • Story ring — active gradient, seen grey, or hidden
  • Verified badge, follow button, and location tag toggles
  • Carousel indicator dots with configurable image count
  • Multi-image badge overlay (e.g. "1/5" in corner)
  • Like heart active state (red filled), bookmark active state
  • Custom comments with username and avatar emoji
  • Full Stories strip in device shell with sample accounts
  • Caption truncation ("…more") toggle
  • 100% private — zero data stored or sent anywhere
